Maximize your battery lifespan with key features (100% free)

Charge Limiter

Battery Toolkit lets you set a custom charge limit (e.g. 80%) to prevent overcharging and optimize battery life. Works for Apple's Intel and Silicon Macs.

Sailing Mode

Battery Toolkit prevents short charging levels by only charging your Mac when it reaches minimum (20% or higher) charging threshold.

Discharge

Allows your MacBook to run completely on battery power even if it's plugged in. This can help maintain battery health.

MagSafe Light

Signal the power state of your Macbook to the MagSafe indicator light. This visual feedback helps you understand more about your Mac's power status.
